Welcome to Sitka Jeep Rentals — your gateway to adventure in beautiful Sitka, Alaska. We specialize in renting rugged, reliable Jeep Wranglers designed to handle the scenic drives and off-the-beaten-path destinations unique to Southeast Alaska. Whether you’re a cruise ship passenger, visiting family, or exploring solo, our locally owned and operated business is here to provide flexible, safe, and memorable transportation. We pride ourselves on excellent customer service, clean vehicles, and hassle-free pick-up and drop-off locations. Discover Sitka’s hidden gems at your own pace — from forest trails to coastal views — in the comfort of a Jeep built for the wild.
